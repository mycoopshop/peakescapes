Meet Our Posse

Chris Ezzy
Chief Escape Artist

Chris’ mission is to create awe inspiring and life altering travel experiences for our clients. When he’s not creating bucket list worthy trips, he can be found sourcing providers of spectacular experiences, tours and accommodation. With over 16 years’ experience in travel and tourism, 7 years living abroad in 3 different countries and a travel resume of 40 countries (and counting), Chris is certainly a world class authority on escaping! 

Sam Ezzy
Chief Experience Officer

Sam’s background involved mostly marketing and life coaching before taking off to Canada where she lived in the stunningly beautiful town of Banff for 2 years (and met Chris). It was here her love of nature and bucket list worthy experiences was ignited. She is the Chief Experience Officer (aka Ops Manager) of Peak Escapes and Mumma to 2 spirited kids. Sam’s motto is ‘collect moments not stuff’ and her mission is to support others in doing just that!
